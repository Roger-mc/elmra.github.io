Integrity is alignment of speech, action, and conscience.

What does it mean to be true to yourself?

To be true to yourself is not indulgence, it’s alignment. It is the quiet work of living in step with what you believe is right, even when it’s hard and no one sees.

Perfection is an outdated illusion, let it go. Sanity begins when we accept life on its terms and focus our actions on what truly matters.

Practise truth

  • Stop saying, doing, or accepting things that make you feel weak.
  • Speak without fear and choose your tone carefully to avoid cruelty.
  • Take responsibility for what you believe. Let your values lead.
  • Don’t perform for others look inward for direction.
  • And when you forget, begin again. That too is truth.
