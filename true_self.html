What Does It Mean to Be True to your self?

Truth at Elmra is not rigid or flawless. It is a daily alignment of word, action, and value. It calls for courage: seeing your shadow, speaking plainly, and living by what matters.

Truth neither hides nor harms. It is justice without judgment, honesty without pride, conviction with compassion.

Practise truth

  • Speak when silence feels safe.
  • Repair the harm you cause.
  • Write your core values.
  • Choose clarity over control.
