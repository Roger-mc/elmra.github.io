What Does It Mean to Be True to your self?

Truth at Elmra is not rigid or flawless. It is daily alignment of words and actions to your values. It calls for courage, speaking plainly, and being true to what matters.

Truth neither hides nor harms. Practice justice without judgment and honesty with compassion.

Practise truth

  • Choose simplicity and clarity over complexity.
  • Reflect on your goals and core values.
  • Speak up when silence feels safe.
